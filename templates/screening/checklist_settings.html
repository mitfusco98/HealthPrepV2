Configuration Guidelines
Data Cutoff Recommendations
  • Lab Results: 12-18 months for most screenings
  • Imaging Studies: 24-36 months due to longer intervals
  • Consult Reports: 12 months for active care
  • Hospital Records: 24-36 months for significant events
OCR Confidence Guidelines
  • 0.8-1.0: High confidence, reliable text
  • 0.6-0.8: Medium confidence, generally accurate
  • 0.0-0.6: Low confidence, may contain errors
  • Lower thresholds include more documents but with potential inaccuracies
