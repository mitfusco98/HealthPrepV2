## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes requirements for security awareness and training to ensure all personnel understand their responsibilities for protecting HealthPrep systems and Protected Health Information (PHI). It addresses HIPAA Security Rule requirements (45 CFR 164.308(a)(5)) and HITRUST CSF controls.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:
- All HealthPrep employees and contractors
- Personnel with access to HealthPrep systems
- Third-party vendors with system access
- Organization administrators using the platform

---

## 3. Roles and Responsibilities

### 3.1 Security Officer
- Develop and maintain training curriculum
- Track training completion and compliance
- Update training materials annually
- Report training metrics to management

### 3.2 Management
- Ensure personnel complete required training
- Allocate time for training activities
- Enforce training compliance

### 3.3 All Personnel
- Complete assigned training within deadlines
- Apply security practices in daily work
- Report security concerns and incidents
- Maintain awareness of current threats

---

## 4. Training Requirements

### 4.1 New Hire Training

All new personnel must complete security awareness training within 30 days of hire or access grant.

| Topic | Duration | Assessment |
|-------|----------|------------|
| HIPAA Privacy and Security Overview | 30 min | Quiz (80% pass) |
| HealthPrep Security Policies | 30 min | Quiz (80% pass) |
| PHI Handling Procedures | 20 min | Quiz (80% pass) |
| Password and Authentication Security | 15 min | Quiz (80% pass) |
| Incident Reporting Procedures | 15 min | Acknowledgment |

**Total New Hire Training**: ~2 hours

### 4.2 Annual Refresher Training

All personnel must complete annual refresher training within their anniversary month.

| Topic | Duration | Assessment |
|-------|----------|------------|
| Security Policy Updates | 20 min | Quiz (80% pass) |
| HIPAA Compliance Refresher | 20 min | Quiz (80% pass) |
| Current Threat Awareness | 20 min | Quiz (80% pass) |
| Incident Response Review | 15 min | Acknowledgment |

**Total Annual Refresher**: ~1.25 hours

### 4.3 Role-Based Training

Additional training based on job function:

| Role | Additional Training | Frequency |
|------|---------------------|-----------|
| System Administrators | Secure configuration, access management | Annual |
| Developers | Secure coding practices, OWASP Top 10 | Annual |
| Organization Admins | User management, audit log review | At onboarding |
| Support Personnel | PHI handling, customer data protection | Annual |

---

## 5. Training Topics

### 5.1 Core Security Topics

**Password Security**
- Strong password creation
- Password manager usage
- Multi-factor authentication
- Account compromise indicators

**Phishing Awareness**
- Email phishing recognition
- Social engineering tactics
- Reporting suspicious messages
- Safe link verification

**PHI Protection**
- Definition of PHI
- Minimum necessary principle
- Secure PHI transmission
- PHI disposal procedures

**Incident Response**
- Security incident recognition
- Reporting procedures
- Escalation contacts
- Evidence preservation

### 5.2 HIPAA-Specific Topics

**Privacy Rule**
- Patient rights
- Use and disclosure limitations
- Authorization requirements
- Minimum necessary standard

**Security Rule**
- Administrative safeguards
- Physical safeguards
- Technical safeguards
- Breach notification requirements

## 7. Compliance Tracking

### 7.1 Training Records

The following records are maintained:

| Record | Retention | Format |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| Training completion | 7 years | LMS database |
| Quiz scores | 7 years | LMS database |
| Attestation signatures | 7 years | PDF/electronic |
| Attendance records | 7 years | Spreadsheet/LMS |

### 7.2 Compliance Reporting

**Monthly**:
- Training completion rates
- Overdue training alerts

**Quarterly**:
- Compliance metrics to management
- Training effectiveness analysis

**Annually**:
- Full compliance audit
- Training program review

### 7.3 Non-Compliance

| Overdue Period | Action |
|----------------|--------|
| 1-14 days | Email reminder to employee and manager |
| 15-30 days | Escalation to department head |
| 30+ days | Access restriction consideration |
| 60+ days | Mandatory remediation, potential disciplinary action |

---

## 8. Training Effectiveness

### 8.1 Assessment Methods

- Quiz scores (minimum 80% to pass)
- Phishing simulation click rates
- Incident reporting rates
- Security audit findings

### 8.2 Improvement Process

1. Review training metrics quarterly
2. Analyze incident trends for training gaps
3. Update curriculum based on findings
4. Incorporate industry threat intelligence
5. Solicit feedback from participants
